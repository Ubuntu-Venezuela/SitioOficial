## Our Social Commitment

At **Ubuntu Venezuela**, we believe that technology and knowledge should be accessible to everyone. We are not just technologists; we believe in the positive social impact that Free Software, Open Source, and free culture have on education and the development of our nation.

### What do we do to fulfill our commitment?
- **Education:** We provide talks, workshops, and technical support to public and private educational institutions, promoting open tools as a superior alternative.
- **Digital Inclusion:** We promote lightweight distributions from the Ubuntu family (such as Lubuntu and Xubuntu) to revitalize laboratories and low-resource hardware.
- **Active Participation:** We support national activities (FLISOL, RMS, Software Freedom Day) strengthening alliances with other communities for the benefit of citizens on their path to sovereign technological literacy.

Our name comes from the African philosophical concept *Ubuntu*: **"I am because we are."** This belief defines us: we only grow as professionals if we help our community grow with us.
