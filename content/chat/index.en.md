# Communication Channels

The Ubuntu Venezuela community primarily gathers on **Telegram**, but we also maintain presence on the traditional Ubuntu IRC network.

## Telegram
Our main communication channel is Telegram. It's an open group where you can resolve questions, share news, or simply chat with other Ubuntu users in Venezuela.

{{< button text="Join the Telegram group" href="https://t.me/ubuntuve" icon="external-link" >}}

## IRC (Libera.chat)
You can find us on the `irc.libera.chat` server, in the `#ubuntu-ve` channel.

- **Server:** irc.libera.chat
- **Channel:** #ubuntu-ve
- **Port (TLS):** 6697

> *Please note that all conversation history in Ubuntu IRC channels is logged by the `ubuntulog` bot. You can view the history at irclogs.ubuntu.com.*
