description: >-
   The Ubuntu Venezuela Community (Ubuntu-ve) is an official Ubuntu Local community (LoCo Team). 
   Founded in late 2005, our mission is to stimulate and support the use of Ubuntu GNU/Linux as a free, reliable, and secure alternative in Venezuela. 
   We educate users in schools and universities based on the freedom of knowledge.
   We organize install-fests, events like FLISoL, and provide continuous support through our online community platforms.
