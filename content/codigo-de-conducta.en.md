## Ubuntu Code of Conduct

The Ubuntu Venezuela community is built on mutual respect, collaboration, and courtesy.
Guided by the [official Ubuntu Code of Conduct](https://ubuntu.com/community/ethos/code-of-conduct), we ensure a healthy environment, free from harassment, where learning flows freely.

### Our Core Principles:
1. **Be considerate:** Our work will be used and affect other people.
2. **Be respectful:** Disagreement is no excuse for disrespect or rudeness.
3. **Be collaborative:** Collaboration and teamwork are the core of our success.
4. **When in doubt, consult:** We resolve disagreements with maturity.

If you experience or witness behavior that violates these rules within Ubuntu Venezuela's spaces (digital or physical), please contact us directly at **junta@ubuntu-ve.org**.
